Artwork Requirements

  1. Create a piece of artwork that is related to Chinese culture. E.g. Chinese fashion, landscape, history, etc.
  2. Artwork can be both paper or digital, but must be submitted online in jpg or png format with high quality resolution.
  3. Artwork must be respectful and appropriate.
