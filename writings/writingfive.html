The End

By:Christina Fan


Everything followed its usual path
Life drove its way through the road of repeat
The journey as predictable as math
But the change that came was not so discreet

Life began to follow an unknown track
The people are mice, they now all stay home
They go out to stack their sac and come back
It is no longer an option to roam

I wonder when this is going to end
I wonder if it’s lasting forever
How much time will it take for us to spend
To continue this long, hard endeavour

Now I sit and wonder what will happen
Now I sit and wonder what will happen

还是永远如此下去了
我们还要花多少时间
来继续把这个漫长，艰难的路走完

我坐着想象着未来
